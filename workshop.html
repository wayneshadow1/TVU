Our Signature Diversity and Inclusivity Workshop

The

Diversity and Inclusivity workshop
is is our signature workshop designed by us to Enhance,EducateandEnrich Society

WorkShop details and Schedule

Detail Description
Duration -3 hrs per day for 3 days(9 hours)
Address -To be Decided Upon Contact
Who Should Attend -Human Resource (HR) Managers and Executives
-Managers, Team Leaders and Executives working with PWDs.
Goals -To bridge the gap between company and PWDs
-The training package teaches companies how to engage PWDs and develop a more inclusive workplace culture for those with cerebral palsy.
Outconmes -Employers will have a better understanding of the needs and wants of their employees with cerebral palsy and be able to implement better infrastructure to their environment to prevent discrimination.
-Communicate effectively and create a welcoming workplace for PWDs
-Use real-world advice for hiring requirements, interviewing, and integrating PWDs into the workplace.
-Provide workplace accommodations, employee involvement and support for PWDs

Introduction and understanding

In this session , people with cerebal palsy will speak on their various symptomps and how it can impede their work. Consequently , sharing their experience with how discrimination , exclusion and inaccessibility exacerbates and their condition

Take Space, Make Space

Attendees roleplay those with cerebral palsy by doing simple tasks with impairment much like how those with cerebral palsy might have difficulty doing like typing without assistive Technologynology, going about an inaccesible space in a wheel chair etc. This session aims to build empathy between Non-PWDS and PWDs viscerally

Brainstorming & Discussion

Attendees break into groups and brainstorm ideas to make their workplace,culture and environment more inclusive from self practices to larger canpaigns and present them to the larger group.

Making a more accessible workplace

Facilitators go through the suggestions and go through their own observations of the company as to how to improve its conditions

Diversity In the Workplace

Persons with cerebral palsy will share personal stories and statistics of the struggles PWDs face with gaining employment

Empath Exercise

Attendees roleplay real world scenarios of job interviews, facing daily exclusion and being fired for not being quick enough through a series of elimination games designed for them to fail

Empathy Exercise 2.0

attendees will participate in the same activities with a buddy . this time the game is targeted for them to succeed with assistance

Implementing Diversity in the Workplace

Facilitators introduce resources and support systems that attendees can implement into their workplace to make the company more diverse in its hires and culture

Networking and Dismissal

Attendees and facilitators are given a chance to work together and get to know each other

Assistive Technologies

Facilitators will present assistive Technology that help them and how in the workplace

Assistive Technology Companies Talk

Companies that specialize in assistive Technology will be giving 10 minutes each to present their offers or packages

Networking and Dismissal

Attendeesm will be able to network with the assistive Technology companies

Closing speech and ceremony
