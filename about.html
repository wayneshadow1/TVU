About us

"Our team name is entitled 'The Visionaries United' and we are promoting inclusivity in our society. We aim to help people with physical disabilities such as cerebral palsy to find a suitable job that is in the society. Therefore we plan workshops that helps to convey a message to all employers out there as well as co-workers to encourage them that people diagnosed with disabilities have the potential to excel in their workplace as well!"
